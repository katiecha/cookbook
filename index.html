  • Bibimbap • $14

    A beloved Korean classic meaning "mixed rice." A warm bowl of steamed rice is topped with an array of seasoned vegetables, a perfectly fried egg, and a drizzle of gochujang chili paste.

    Served in a sizzling stone bowl (dolsot) so the rice crisps at the bottom. Choose your protein: bulgogi beef, spicy pork, tofu, or mushroom. Mix it all together before the first bite!

  • Bulgogi • $18

    Thinly sliced beef marinated for 24 hours in a blend of soy sauce, Asian pear, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil, then grilled over high heat for a caramelized char.

    Bulgogi — literally "fire meat" — is one of Korea's most iconic dishes. Served with steamed rice, banchan side dishes, and crisp lettuce leaves for wrapping.

  • Pajeon • $12

    Great for sharing.

    Crispy Korean savory pancakes packed with fresh scallions and your choice of add-ins: seafood (shrimp & squid), kimchi, or mushroom & zucchini.

    Served golden-brown and sliced, alongside a housemade soy-vinegar dipping sauce. The ideal starter or side for any Korean meal.
